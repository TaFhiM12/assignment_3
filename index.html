Different Dogs

German Shepherd

Known for their intelligence and loyalty, German Shepherds are excellent working dogs often used in police and military roles. They are highly trainable and make great family pets.

Beagle

Beagles are friendly, curious, and great with kids. They have an excellent sense of smell and are often used as detection dogs. Their playful nature makes them wonderful companions.

Bulldog

Bulldogs are calm, courageous, and have a distinctive wrinkled face. They are known for their gentle temperament and make excellent companions for families and individuals alike.
